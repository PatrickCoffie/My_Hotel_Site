About The Grand Haven Hotel

Welcome to The Grand Haven Hotel, your luxurious home away from home in Ghana. We pride ourselves on exceptional service, elegant rooms, and world-class amenities. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our hotel ensures comfort, style, and a memorable experience.

Our facilities include a swimming pool, spa, fine dining restaurant, and conference halls. Every detail has been designed with our guests in mind, creating a perfect balance of relaxation and sophistication.
